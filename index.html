The Recipe

How Sana decides what your body needs today

Most apps give you a static program. Sana does the opposite: it reads four physiological signals every morning and places your body into one of four states, each with its own protocol stack.

The classification logic is transparent and deterministic — no black box. You can follow the same decision tree Sana uses.

If you reported heavy social activity the night before, a fifth overlay activates — a dedicated recovery protocol that addresses alcohol's specific metabolic effects without judgment.

🟢 Green Day
Energy ≥ 4 · Soreness ≤ 2 · Sleep ≥ 7.5h
→ Full performance. Max intensity, high calories, full supplement stack.
🔵 Standard
No extreme flags triggered
→ Balanced training, maintenance nutrition, standard stack.
🟡 Light
Low energy, high soreness, or poor sleep
→ Reduced volume, recovery nutrition, support stack.
🔴 Rest
Low recovery AND high stress (both)
→ Mobility only, anti-inflammatory focus, cortisol management.
🌙 Social Overlay
Moderate or heavy social activity logged → Appears on top of whichever base state applies. Addresses hydration recovery, liver support, sleep quality restoration, and electrolyte repletion.
How it Reads

Six signals. One clear picture.

Sana weighs six subjective signals from your daily check-in. If you've connected a wearable, objective HRV, sleep staging, and readiness scores layer in as a second source of truth.

When signals conflict — say your wearable says high readiness but you rated energy at 2 — Sana errs on the side of caution. The conservative path protects long-term adaptation.

Supported wearables: Apple Watch, Oura Ring, Whoop, Garmin, Google Fit, Fitbit. No device? The check-in alone works great.

  • ⚡
    Energy Level
    1–5 scale. The single most important signal. Below 2 triggers the low-recovery flag regardless of other inputs.
  • 😰
    Stress Level
    1–5 scale. At 4+ it activates the high-stress flag. Combined with low recovery = REST state.
  • 🟡
    Soreness
    1–5 scale. At 4+ it independently triggers the low-recovery flag and reduces training volume.
  • 🌙
    Sleep Hours
    Grid-picked in 30-min increments. Under 6h triggers low-recovery. Over 7.5h contributes to the GREEN state.
  • 🥂
    Social Context
    None / Light / Moderate / Heavy. Moderate or heavy activates the Social recovery overlay on the dashboard.
  • 😊
    Mood
    1–5 scale. Informs supplement and sleep protocol tone. Does not directly trigger state changes.
Protocols

Five pillars. Total coverage.

Each pillar adapts independently to your state. A REST day doesn't mean skipping everything — it means optimizing each pillar for recovery.

🏋️
Training
Load, volume, and exercise selection adapt daily. REST days become mobility + breathwork. GREEN days unlock full intensity. Built around your equipment, fitness level, and goals.
🍽️
Nutrition
Calories and protein shift with your state. High-output days = performance fueling. Recovery days = anti-inflammatory focus. Personalized to your body, sex, and goal.
💊
Supplements
Four evidence-backed supplements, dosed to your signals. Creatine on training days. Ashwagandha on high-stress days. Magnesium every night. No blends, no guesswork.
😴
Sleep
Wind-down protocol adapts to your sleep debt and last night's activity. Short-sleep days start earlier. Alcohol nights get REM-restoration guidance. Environment + timing matter as much as hours.
🌙
Social
Only activates when you log a night out. Hydration, B-vitamins, liver support, reduced training load — a full recovery plan without the judgment.

The Research

Built on science, not trends.

Every protocol maps to a body of peer-reviewed research. These are the five fields that power the system.

💤
Sleep & Recovery
Slow-wave sleep drives growth hormone release. REM regulates mood and consolidates adaptation. Below 6 hours, testosterone drops 10–15% and cortisol rises — directly opposing performance.
JAMA · Sleep Medicine Reviews · Journal of Neuroscience
🏋️
Strength & Adaptation
Progressive overload, sufficient volume, and recovery are the three non-negotiables for muscle development. High stress and poor sleep suppress adaptation — Sana adjusts your load before you walk in, not after.
Journal of Strength & Conditioning Research · Sports Medicine
💊
Supplementation
Four supplements have robust clinical evidence: Creatine monohydrate, Vitamin D3, Magnesium glycinate, Ashwagandha KSM-66. Sana uses only these — at studied doses, timed to your daily signals.
ISSN Position Stands · NEJM · Current Pharmaceutical Design
🧠
Stress & Hormones
Chronic stress elevates cortisol, which suppresses testosterone, disrupts sleep, and slows recovery. Adaptogens, breathwork, and load management are evidence-backed levers Sana uses on high-stress days.
BMC Endocrine Disorders · Psychoneuroendocrinology
🌙
Social Recovery
Alcohol suppresses REM by up to 25%, depletes B-vitamins and electrolytes, and metabolises into acetaldehyde. NAC, cysteine-rich foods, and rehydration are the interventions Sana deploys after social nights.
Alcohol Research & Health · PLOS Medicine · Current Drug Abuse Reviews
